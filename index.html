What’s Padel?

Imagine tennis, but more social. Smaller courts, fast rallies, and walls in play. It’s the fastest-growing racquet sport in the world — and it’s about to take over British Columbia.

Why VanPadel?

We’re not just building a court—we’re building a community. Whether you're new to the game or a seasoned player, VanPadel is your home base for connection, improvement, and fun.

  • 🏟️ Indoor courts designed for year-round play
  • 📱 App-based booking & skill matching
  • 🌐 Play solo or find partners instantly
  • 🎉 Weekly events, leagues, and open mixers
  • 💬 Welcoming, inclusive community
